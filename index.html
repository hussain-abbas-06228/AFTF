Most people wear sunscreen to protect their skin from painful, red, sunburns; however, did you know that sunscreen can also help protect you from heat exhaustion or heat stroke? Heat exhaustion and heat stroke symptoms include nausea, muscle cramps and, in extreme cases, can even result in brain damage.
Using our SPF Store, you can order SPF sunscreen from the comfort of your home.
